My favourite places
Bagan, Myanmar

It's hard to truly grasp the magnitude of Myanmar's Bagan Archaeological Zone,
dominated by thousands of temples, pagodas, and stupas. Explore the UNESCO World Heritage Site on a bike,
or for an eagle's-eye view, take a hot-air balloon ride at sunrise to see the temples scattered across the lush landscape.
Railay Beach,Thailand

Railay Beach is considered one of the mesmerising and beautiful places in the world because of its natural placement. Isolated by the mainland by limestone cliffs and surrounded by dense jungle, Railay beach's beauty is unmatchable to any other destination in Thailand. No wonder, Railay Beach is so stunning and laidback, making it counted among the beautiful locations in the world.
Cappadocia, Turkey

The pristine wonders of Turkey include many beautiful places like Istanbul, Pool of Abraham and Cappadocia. Among these places, there are so many cultural surprises hidden everywhere which are worth exploring and spending your time. As one of the most amazing tourists places in the world, Cappadocia attracts many visitors every year.
Milford Sound,New Zealand

According to Rudyard Kipling, Milford Sound is the eighth wonder of the world. And if you pay a visit to this region, you will have all the reasons to believe this. Dotted with breathtaking landscapes and cliffs rising from fjords, Milford Sound was formed by glaciers during the ice age. Today, this place is among favourite tourist destinations in the world for anyone who wishes to take a peek into the geographical wonders that time can create.
Santorini, Greece

Tell anyone about your trip to Greece and the first name they'll suggest will be Santorini. Counted among marvellous tourist destinations in the world with amazing heritage and culture, Santorini is worth considering for your honeymoon trip. Santorini in Greece is gaining a lot of popularity among travellers. With a sea-drowned caldera as the main feature and beautiful smaller islands in the background, Santorini surely is one of the most beautiful places in the world.
Munnar, India

Kerala in India is where Munnar is located. Surrounded by amazing and gorgeous Western Ghats, the tea gardens of Munnar make it one of the tourist places in world worth visiting. There is no other best places to travel in the world unlike the scenic paradise called Munnar.The beautiful tea plantations on the rolling mountains of Munnar attracts millions of tourists every year. A ride on the snaking roads of Munnar is a delightful experience.

Ha Long Bay,Vietnam

With a spectacular scenery formed by floating villages, multiple caves, islets, beguiling limestone formations, several hidden beaches and lovely arches, Ha Long Bay in Vietnam surely deserves the title of one of the most Beautiful Places in the World. While the months from April to June turn the waters a striking emerald green, October and November showcase the magical side of bay turning the view that can be best described as 'eerily charming'.
Serengeti National Park,Tanzania

The vast expanse of lands scattered with innumerable beautiful animals makes Serengeti National Park in Tanzania a magnificent place to visit. The hot equatorial sun renders the land a glowing orange hue that's too prominent to miss, while the free jungle creatures like giraffes, zebras, leopards, rhinos and so on, make the perfect models for brilliant safari and wildlife photography.
